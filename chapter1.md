# Grumpy in Wonderland
In a quiet, blue forest lived a tiny gnome named Grumpy. Grumpy was not like the other gnomes. While they often stayed hidden, he loved to explore. Grumpy had a bright red hat, a long white beard, and twinkling eyes that sparkled with curiosity.

One sunny day, Grumpy decided to wander deeper into the forest than ever before. The trees were tall, and the sunlight danced through the leaves, creating funny shadows on the ground. As he walked, he heard sweet birds singing and saw colorful flowers blooming everywhere.
