# Suprise!
Suddenly, a loud rumble came from the sky. Dark clouds formed, and it started to rain heavily. Grumpy grabbed a handful of treasure and ran. He slipped into a small cave nearby for safety. Grumpy realized that the real treasure was not just the gold but the adventure he had, the friends he made, and the stories he would tell. 

When the storm passed, Grumpy returned home, his heart full of happiness and stories to share with all the other gnomes.
