# The misterious key
After a while, Jolly spotted something shiny. It was a beautiful, golden key, lying on the soft moss. Jolly picked it up, wondering where it could belong. His heart raced with excitement. What if it opened a secret door? With this thought in mind, he continued walking, looking for a door to match the key.

He came across a wise old owl sitting on a branch. “Ho, ho! What do you have there, little gnome?” asked the owl.
